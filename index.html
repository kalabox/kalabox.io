    Kalabox is the missing piece of your development toolchain. Think of it as a free server running on your laptop that, instead of speaking sysadmin, talks to you in a soothing Barry White baritone. To start getting funky with the box, simply...

    Easy Installation

    Download the appropriate package for your Mac, Windows, or Linux computer, decompress the download, and double click on the Kalabox application. The Kalabox installer will get you grooving quick with VirtualBox, Docker, Drush, PHPMyAdmin, Terminus, WPConsole, and a whole lot more.

    Pull Sites From Pantheon

    Now that you're at the Kalabox dashboard, click "Add Site" and select "Pantheon" from the sidebar menu to gain access to your sites on Pantheon. Don't have a Pantheon account? Get a free one! If you think you'll never use Pantheon, don't despair: we'll be adding other providers and non-provider connected sites soon.

    Develop on Your Computer

    After your site is downloaded, it should show on your dashboard. Click the "Start" button. Success! Now just click on the site image or name to visit your site in the browser, or start editing code by selecting "files" from the site options dropdown.

    Started in Seconds
