Kalabox is the missing piece of your development toolchain. Think of it as a free server running on your laptop that, instead of speaking sysadmin, talks to you in a soothing Barry White baritone. To start getting funky with the box, simply...

Easy Installation

Kalabox installs like any other application: download it, drag it to your applications folder, and double-click. No command line instructions (although there is a CLI), no battling dependencies and messing around with folder permissions.

  • Compatible with Mac, Windows, and Linux.
  • Virtualbox, Docker, Drush, PHPMyAdmin, Terminus, and more installed hastle-free. Never worry about dependencies.
  • Fast page loads with Docker.

Create Sites Effortlessly

Kalabox integrates with the Pantheon hosting platform, allowing you to pull down your sites with the press of a button. Don't have a Pantheon account? Get a free one! If you think you'll never use Pantheon, don't despair: we'll be adding other providers and non-provider connected sites soon.

  • Pull down and deploy to your Pantheon sites.
  • Manage sites on an intuitive dashboard.
  • Site environments mirror Pantheon's MySQL, PHP, Solr, Redis, and Nginx versions.

Develop With Your Tools

Working with Kalabox is easy: all your application's files are on your computer, accessible by your favorite IDE or text editor. Press the "Start" button on your site and it's available in your browser. Want more power? Kalabox is powered by an open-source framework that you can extend and customize to your needs.

  • Use your favorite desktop text editor or IDE.
  • Enjoy pre-packaged tools like Drush, WPConsole, Terminus, xdebug, PHPMYadmin, and more.
  • Open source framework allows extension and customization.
